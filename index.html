So far, I have produced the general structure of my interactive article webpage. I have decided the topic/question I want to address with my exploratory visualizations. The most challenging aspect of my project to design will be making the page dynamic rather than purely static. I will try to use elements we learned throughout the quarter to make the article as interactive and engaging as possible. I am still brainstorming ways to incorporate engagement and interaction.
